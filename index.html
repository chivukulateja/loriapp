Lend Out Rent In

Lori is an online Peer to Peer lending and borrowing platform that allown people to rent and borrow things in minutes away.

How to list?

Register on LORI.
List the product that you wish to lend by keying in the required information. LORI shall review the same to ensure that it qualifies with all the compliances and take the product live. Your listing is now publicly visible. Cheer$!

How to lend ?

Confirmation Renter pays the rent to LORI as confirmation Lend On the day, lend the item to the renter after confirming that it was lent in promised condition Earn Upon completion of rental cycle, provide feedback and request to ‘collect’ your earned amount, and the same shall be credited to you.

How to rent (borrow)?

Type what you are looking for or search for it via applying various filters Request for approval Sent in a request to rent the item by agreeing on the ‘hire fee’ & terms & conditions,and then await approval for the same from the lender Pay to confirm Upon receiving confirmation from lender, pay the ‘hire fee + rental fee’ to confirm rental of the item Pick up & return You can either collect and return the item yourself from the lender as we will connect the two parties after collecting ‘hire fee’ or you can request for a pick up & drop of the rented item through our delivery partners for an extra delivery fee.

How to request a product?

We care about your needs.Upon searching for the product if you do not find it, then simply write to us about what you are looking for and we shall inform the LORI community around you to help you by listing & renting the product out to you if they have it.
